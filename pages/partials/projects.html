Arduino LED VU-Meter

LED tower that reads input from a microphone and lights up its rows corresponding to the volume level. Passes the audio signal through an RC low-pass filter to give precedence to the lower frequencies (bass) of received signals. Contolled with an Adafruit 5V Metro Mini, programmed in C.

TA Emergency Coverage System

Web application that allows teaching assitants to easily find a replacement when they cannot teach a lab section. Built using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, Jquery, AJAX, PHP, and MySQL.

Portable Phone Charger

Portable phone charger designed on Autodesk EAGLE, printed on a circuit board, and soldered all together. Housed in a custom case made from 3D-printed and laser-cut parts.

LED Inifinity Table

Table with a regular mirror on the bottom and an acrylic one-way mirror on the top that makes it look like it descends infinitely when the lights are turned on. Contolled with an Adafruit 5V Trinket, programmed in C.
